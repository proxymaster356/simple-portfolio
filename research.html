Biotechnology Research

Current research in genetic engineering and bioprocess optimization holds great promise for medical advancements and industrial efficiency. Benefits include targeted disease treatments, improved crop yields, and streamlined biopharmaceutical production. However, ethical concerns, biosafety regulations, environmental impacts, and equitable access remain critical considerations.

Interest in Key Areas:

  • Protein Engineering

Future Projects:
